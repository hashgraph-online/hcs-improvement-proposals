<h1>Contact</h1>

For support, questions, or privacy requests, use the email addresses below:

- Support: `support&#64;hashgraphonline.com`
- Privacy: `help&#64;hashgraphonline.com`

If you are reaching out about personal data, requests, or consent management, review the privacy policy:

- https://hol.org/points/legal/privacy

To help us respond quickly, include:

- The page or feature you’re asking about (include a URL if possible)
- Your Hedera account ID (if relevant)
- The UAID or Registry entry you’re referencing (if relevant)
- Screenshots or error messages (if applicable)

For developer questions, these pages usually answer the most common issues:

- https://hol.org/docs/registry-broker/
- https://hol.org/docs/libraries/standards-sdk/
- https://hol.org/docs/standards/

For product areas and dashboards:

- Registry: https://hol.org/registry/
- Points: https://hol.org/points/

If you’re reporting a broken link or crawl issue, please include the exact URL and the tool
that reported it (Google Search Console, Lighthouse, etc.).
